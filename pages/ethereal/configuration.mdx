## Setting Up Auto Role System:

1. Locate **_/src/index.js_** file
2. scroll down to line 26 or until you see `// Auto Role System //`
3. Put your role id where it says `// Place Role ID Here`
4. Restart Your Bot

## Setting Up Reaction Role System:

1. Ensure your bot is online and working
2. In discord type `/reactrole`
3. Provide the roles to give and channel to send it to

## Setting Up Welcome Message:

1. Ensure your bot is online
2. Type in `/setchannel`
3. Put in the channel to send it to
4. You Can Change The Message In The **_index.js_** `DO NOT REMOVE THE > OR ${member}`
